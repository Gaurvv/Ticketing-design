Important Notes / महत्वपूर्ण जानकारी

Rules and Regulations

  1. This permit is not transferable and not refundable.
  2. Rubbish must be placed out.
  3. Lighting fire is prohibited.
  4. Movement between sunset to sunrise is prohibited.
  5. The permit is for one person per entry only.
  6. Guide is must for jungle walk.
  7. Collecting any product is prohibited.
  8. Please clean camping grounds before you leave.
